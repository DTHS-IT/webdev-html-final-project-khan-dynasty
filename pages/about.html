About

Terence Khan is a skilled handyman based in Newark, DE, known for his expertise in home repairs and maintenance. With years of experience in the industry, Terence has built a reputation for delivering high-quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

Whether it's fixing a leaky faucet, repairing drywall, or cutting grass, Terence approaches each project with professionalism and attention to detail. His commitment to excellence ensures that every job is completed to the satisfaction of his clients.

In addition to his technical skills, Terence is also known for his reliability. He understands the importance of respecting his clients' time and property, making him a trusted choice for homeowners.

When he's not busy helping clients with their home improvement needs, Terence enjoys spending time with his family and exploring the outdoors.
